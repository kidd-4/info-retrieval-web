class="wpb_wrapper">\n\t\t\t<h3>Rock Demers</h3>\n<p>Founder (in 1980) and President of \u201cLes Productions La\xa0F\xeate\u201d and creator of <em>CONTES POUR TOUS</em>, Rock Demers has been involved in all facets of cinema (critiquing, distribution, export, operation, production). In early 50\u2019s, he co-founded the magazine <em>Images</em> and established many film clubs. In 1958, after having studied teaching, he quit the country in order to travel across Europe and Asia for two years. On return to Canada, he joined the team that had just established the International Film Festival of Montreal. He became its Director General from 1962 to 1967. He was one of the co-founders of\xa0Cin\xe9math\xe8que qu\xe9b\xe9coise in 1963 and established Faroun Films in 1965. In 1968 he devoted himself to Faroun Films, whose distribution of films for the youth rapidly expanded to more than ten countries. With the export of the film\xa0<em>Martien de No\xebl</em> and other films, such as\xa0<em>Les M\xe2les</em> (Gilles Carle), <em>La Vie r\xeav\xe9e</em> (Mireille Dansereau) and <em>Les Smattes</em> (Jean-Claude Labrecque),\xa0Faroun Films became the most important ambassador abroad for the nascent film industry of Quebec. Thanks to the brilliant success of the initial <em>CONTES POUR TOUS</em>, whose exceptional quality was appreciated by the public and the critics (215 national and international awards in under 30 years), the collection now consists of 24 titles and has brought Rock Demers numerous prizes, both national and international, among which are Prix Albert Tessier (Quebec), Companion of the Order of Canada and Chevalier des Arts et des Lettres (France).</p>\n\n\t\t</div> \n\t</div> <div class="separator normal " style="margin-top: 30px;margin-bottom: 30px;background-color: #cccccc;"></div>\n\n\t<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">\n\t\t<div class="wpb_wrapper">\n\t\t\t<h3>Dr. Dushyant Yajnik</h3>\n<p>Initially a family physician, he studied and has specialized in psychotherapy and psychoanalysis.
